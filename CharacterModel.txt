⚔️ Core Character Stats (the essentials)

These define how a character performs in combat:

Strength (STR)
Affects melee damage, carrying capacity, breaking objects.
Dexterity (DEX)
Determines speed, evasion, accuracy, turn order.
Constitution (CON)
Governs health, stamina, resistance to poison/disease.
Intelligence (INT)
Boosts magic damage, learning ability, spell efficiency.
Wisdom (WIS)
Affects mana pool, healing power, perception.
Charisma (CHA) (optional but useful)
Influences dialogue, trading, leadership buffs.
❤️ Combat-Specific Stats

These are derived or secondary stats used during battle:

HP (Health Points) – how much damage you can take
MP (Mana Points) – resource for spells
Stamina / Energy – used for physical abilities
Attack Power – damage output (often based on STR or DEX)
Defense – reduces incoming physical damage
Magic Attack / Magic Defense – for spell interactions
Speed / Initiative – determines turn order
Accuracy – chance to hit
Evasion – chance to dodge
Critical Chance / Damage – chance for bonus damage
🛡️ Advanced / Flavor Stats (optional but adds depth)

Use these if you want more complexity or unique builds:

Luck
Affects crits, loot drops, random events
Faith
Enhances holy magic or resistance to dark magic
Endurance
Impacts stamina regen, armor effectiveness
Perception
Detect traps, hidden enemies, ambush avoidance
